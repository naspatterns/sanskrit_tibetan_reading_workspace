Vajracchedikā Prajñāpāramitā — Chapter 1

evaṃ mayā śrutam ekasmin samaye bhagavān śrāvastyāṃ viharati sma
jetavane anāthapiṇḍadasyārāme mahatā bhikṣusaṃghena sārdham
ardhatrayodaśabhir bhikṣuśataiḥ

atha khalu bhagavān pūrvāhṇakālasamaye nivāsya pātracīvaram ādāya
śrāvastīṃ mahānagarīṃ piṇḍāya prāvikṣat

atha khalu bhagavān śrāvastīṃ mahānagarīṃ piṇḍāya caritvā
kṛtabhaktakṛtyaḥ paścādbhaktapiṇḍapātapratikrāntaḥ pādau prakṣālya
nyaṣīdat prajñapta evāsane paryaṅkam ābhujya ṛjuṃ kāyaṃ praṇidhāya
pratimukhīṃ smṛtim upasthāpya
